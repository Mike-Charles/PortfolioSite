About Me

I am Mike Charles Mapanga, a dedicated professional with a passion for technology, creativity
and service. Currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology in my final
year at the Malawi University of Business and Applied Sciences, I specialize in web design,
UI/UX design and graphics design, combining technical expertise with artistic vision to craft
compelling digital experiences.

As a web designer, I strive to create intuitive and user-friendly websites that not only look
stunning but also deliver seamless functionality. My work in UI/UX design ensures that every
interaction is meaningful, enhancing the connection between users and technology. As a
graphics designer, I bring stories to life through striking visuals that leave a lasting
impression.

Beyond my technical and creative pursuits, I am deeply committed to my faith. As a minister
of the Gospel in music, I use my voice and talent to inspire, uplift, and share a message of
hope and love. My ministry is a reflection of my purpose: to serve others and make a positive
impact in their lives, whether through music or design.

Every project I take on is driven by a desire to bring ideas to life and make a difference in the
world. Let's connect and create something extraordinary together.
